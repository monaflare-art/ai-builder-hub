# China Affiliate Opportunities

Last updated: 2026-06-18

Scope: Alibaba Cloud, Tencent Cloud, Huawei Cloud, Baidu AI Cloud, Volcengine, and JD Cloud. This is a research and prioritization document only. No affiliate applications were submitted.

## Executive Summary

China-friendly cloud affiliate opportunities are currently more promising than new international network applications because several domestic programs support personal real-name accounts and direct CPS-style promotion. The strongest immediate candidates are Tencent Cloud CPS and Alibaba Cloud Cloud Ambassador, followed by Baidu AI Cloud or JD Cloud depending on whether the next sprint prioritizes AI-cloud content fit or fastest operational activation.

Huawei Cloud is not actionable because its reward promotion program is paused from 2026-01-30. Volcengine appears to be a partner/distribution route rather than a lightweight personal affiliate program, so it is better treated as a later B2B partnership opportunity.

## Provider Details

### Tencent Cloud

- Program: Tencent Cloud CPS / 推广大使.
- Official URL: [Tencent Cloud CPS](https://cloud.tencent.com/act/partner/cps).
- Public terms observed:
  - Personal real-name users can participate.
  - Enterprise users, agents, employees, and related partner staff are excluded.
  - Domestic promotion offers 20%-35% cashback on eligible first purchases.
  - International promotion lists fixed 30% cashback on eligible first purchases.
  - Customer association window is 30 days.
  - Commission is calculated monthly and transferred to a bank account in 1-2 months depending on level.
  - Tencent withholds applicable taxes.
- Application requirements:
  - Tencent Cloud account.
  - Personal real-name verification.
  - Bank account for settlement.
  - Compliance with no self-referral and no misleading promotion rules.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Strong fit for VPS, cloud server, domain DNS, and AI builder infrastructure content.
  - Existing tool page present.
- Risk:
  - Domestic identity and settlement setup must be completed by owner.
  - Site needs Chinese-cloud supporting content before traffic can convert.

### Alibaba Cloud

- Program: Alibaba Cloud 云大使.
- Official URL: [Alibaba Cloud Cloud Ambassador](https://dashi.aliyun.com/).
- Public terms observed:
  - Personal and enterprise real-name users can join.
  - Product rebate rates vary by product type. Current public FAQ states cloud resource products can reach 25%, Agent-C products can reach 30%, and other listed product groups vary.
  - Standard association period is 90 days; some AI/model/Agent product purchases in the first 30 days can extend association to 365 days.
  - 云气 is used for rebate accounting and can be converted at 100 云气 = 1 RMB.
  - One valid order is enough to withdraw.
  - Tax is withheld during withdrawal for personal users.
  - Enterprise withdrawal requires enterprise payout information and invoice handling.
- Application requirements:
  - Alibaba Cloud account.
  - Personal or enterprise real-name verification.
  - Withdrawal information.
  - No self-referral or same-person transactions.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Strong fit for Chinese cloud, AI model, VPS, and startup infrastructure content.
  - Existing tool page present.
- Risk:
  - Rules are more complex than Tencent due to product-specific rebate categories and association logic.

### Baidu AI Cloud

- Program: Baidu AI Cloud 云推广大使.
- Official URL: [Baidu AI Cloud Ambassador](https://cloud.baidu.com/campaign/ambassador/index.html).
- Public terms observed:
  - Invite-new-user rebate program with official ambassador signup.
  - Public page states base rebate of 20% and stacked rebate up to 34%.
  - Monthly cap shown as 80,000 RMB, single-user cap 50,000 RMB, and single-order cap 17,000 RMB.
  - Association window for eligible new customers is 30 days.
  - Monthly reward calculation is followed by bank payout after commercial information verification.
  - Rewards are split over four months at 20%, 20%, 20%, and 40%.
- Application requirements:
  - Baidu AI Cloud account.
  - Ambassador application.
  - Commercial information verification and correct bank account.
  - No same-person/self-referral, no agent-related conflict, no refund abuse.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Very good topical fit for AI builders because Baidu AI Cloud includes AI infrastructure and AI services.
  - Existing tool page present.
- Risk:
  - Application and commercial verification details need owner login confirmation.
  - Four-month payout schedule is slower than Tencent/JD.

### JD Cloud

- Program: JD Cloud 云推客.
- Official URLs:
  - [JD Cloud 云推客 overview article](https://developer.jdcloud.com/topics/4270)
  - [JD Cloud 云推客 docs](https://docs.jdcloud.com/cn/yuntuike/guide)
- Public terms observed:
  - Join via JD Cloud 云推客 mini-program flow.
  - Public guide describes scanning the mini-program, logging in, applying for the recommendation rebate plan, agreeing to the online agreement, and activating promotion.
  - Promotion links can lock referred customers.
  - Public article lists monthly policy examples such as up to 40% base cashback during a specific campaign, extended lock period, and extra campaign rewards.
  - Orders can generate estimated commission in the mini-program.
  - Payout generally happens at the end of the following month to the 云推客 account, with withdrawal to bank card.
  - Some campaign rules split high commissions into installments.
- Application requirements:
  - JD Cloud account.
  - WeChat mini-program access.
  - Agreement acceptance.
  - Bank card withdrawal setup.
  - No self-referral or same-person transactions.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Good cloud/server fit, but currently no tool page or related articles.
- Risk:
  - Public policy examples are campaign-specific, so current commission should be verified in the mini-program before promotion.

### Volcengine

- Program: Volcengine Partner Program.
- Official URL: [Volcengine Partner Portal](https://partner.volcengine.com/).
- Public terms observed:
  - The public route is partner-oriented, including retail/distribution/reseller-style cooperation.
  - Program language focuses on partner capabilities, commercial cooperation, distribution, and sales models rather than a lightweight individual affiliate flow.
- Application requirements:
  - Likely company identity, partner qualification, business capability, or solution/sales plan.
  - Personal affiliate eligibility was not found in public pages.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Good future fit for AI infrastructure and China AI cloud coverage.
- Risk:
  - Not a fast first-commission path.
  - Requires partner qualification and probably business-side review.

### Huawei Cloud

- Program: Huawei Cloud 云推官 / reward promotion.
- Official URLs:
  - [Huawei Cloud reward promotion page](https://activity.huaweicloud.com/cps/index.html)
  - [Huawei Cloud pause notice](https://www.huaweicloud.com/notice/2025/20251230101027580.html)
- Public terms observed:
  - Huawei Cloud announced that the reward promotion program pauses from 2026-01-30.
  - New accounts can no longer join the reward promotion plan.
  - New registered accounts no longer associate with 云推官.
  - Existing balances can still be withdrawn.
- Application requirements:
  - Not actionable for new applications.
- Fit for AI Builder Hub:
  - Existing tool page present, but affiliate monetization is unavailable now.
- Risk:
  - Do not prioritize until program reopens or an alternative partner route is identified.
