## Rewrite Principle

Do not rewrite by adding more words. Rewrite by adding:

- Real screenshots.
- Real pricing checks.
- Real setup steps.
- Specific buyer decisions.
- Comparison tables.
- "Avoid if" sections.
- Evidence-backed CTAs.
- Internal links to the matching tool page and competing alternatives.
